Journey To Mordor

How To Play

A great evil looms! You must help Frodo avoid the evil creatures along the way. When you reach the dead marshes, beware of what lurks in the waters! Can you make it to Mount Doom to destroy the ring? Be quick, because time is running out!
Guide Frodo using the arrow keys on your keyboard. To save Middle Earth you must make it to Mount Doom before time runs out!
